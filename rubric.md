My rubric is listed below as an example!
To create your own rubric, first finish the Job Charter (linked in the same repo.)
After creating the Job Charter, run the following prompt: Please create a rubric that scores job postings from 0-10 based on the five criteria you identify as the most important aspects of my job search charter. First, identify those criteria for me to approve. Next, create detailed scoring criteria. Finally, I will submit several job postings for test scoring.

### Role level & scope (0–2 points)
2 = Senior PM / Staff PM / Group PM / Head of X / GM of a product line; owns a clearly defined area, 0–4 directs.
1 = PM or VP‑ish role that’s close but either slightly under or over the scope you want.
0 = Very junior IC, or CPO/VP with broad org‑wide ownership.

### Lifestyle fit & boundaries (0–2 points)
2 = Remote‑first, US‑time‑zone friendly, meetings in roughly 9–5 ET, no “always on” language.​
1 = Heavy travel, “startup hustle,” or vague hours with availability needed in European time zones.
0 = On‑site, frequent travel, or explicit “nights/weekends,” on‑call.

### Company stage & success potential (0–2 points)
2 = Clear stage and traction: Series B to Series C+, mentions real revenue, growth, or strong funding; product not existentially pre‑PMF.​
1 = Series A, or mature but stagnant/unclear.
0 = No evidence of funding, traction, or viability.

### Product / domain fit (0–2 points)
2 = Web3/crypto infra, data/analytics, marketplaces, SaaS with complex systems or mechanisms, adtech/martech with strong AI features.
1 = Adjacent but less exciting domain (eg: legacy SaaS)
0 = Domain you don’t care about or actively want to avoid.

### Culture & org signals (0–2 points)
2 = 30% or more of leadership team are women
1 = Mentions low politics, collaboration, strong product/eng leadership, support for parents/flexibility, or similar.​
0 = Red flags (hero culture, chaos, “rockstar/ninja,” politics) or no info.

### Hard filters — must pass ALL to be included
A role is excluded immediately if any of the following are true. Do not score it.
Geography / work authorization: Role is not open to US-based candidates, or has a hard country requirement (e.g. "must be located in Argentina, Brazil, Spain, or Uruguay") even if labeled "remote"
Salary: Listed or estimated base salary is below $200K. Roles at $220K+ are preferred; flag anything in the $200–219K range as worth verifying
Role type: Not a full-time, permanent, salaried role with benefits — excludes contract, 1099/freelance, part-time, and internship roles
Company stage: Seed-stage company with no meaningful funding, revenue, or traction signal — pre-Series A with no evidence of PMF
Industry: Healthcare, gaming, or gambling — exclude regardless of domain or tech stack
Domain experience: Role does not require fintech experience specifically
